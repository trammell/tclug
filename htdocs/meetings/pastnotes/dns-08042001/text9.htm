PTR

  • Not in your domain's map.

  • Must be delegated from upstream ISP. Usually isn't and they do it themselves for you.

  • Administered by IANA and ARIN in the US.

  • Take the addresses, put them in reverse, add in-addr.arpa and you have your 'reverse ip name'

  • 207.195.212.241 is 241.212.195.207.in-addr.arpa
