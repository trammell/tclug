Ipv6 PTR

  • Different than Ipv4 reverse maps.

  • Take the address, reverse it, delimit each hex number with a dot, and at 'ip6.int' to the end.

  • For example: 3ffe:1200:3028:8bf6:2a0:ccff:fe73:dfa0

  • In map for 6.f.b.8.8.2.0.3.0.0.2.1.e.f.f.3.ip6.int:

    • 0.a.f.d.3.7.e.f.f.f.c.c.0.a.2.0 IN PTR teela.ringworld.org.
